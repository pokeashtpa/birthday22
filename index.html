💌 A Birthday Letter For You 💌

Happy Birthday

My Dearest Leafa, 💙

I don't even know where to begin, because how do you properly celebrate someone who has quietly become such a huge part of your world? ✨

This day isn't just another date on the calendar. It's the day the person who changed my routines, my nights, my thoughts, and even parts of my heart — was born. It's the day the world was blessed with you — and somehow, against all odds, I was blessed enough to meet you too. 🌟

It still amazes me how we built something so real without ever meeting yet. No physical presence. No hand to hold. No birthday hug today. 💭

And yet somehow, what we share feels deeper than most things people build face to face.

We built this through words. ✍️

Through paragraphs. 📝

Through long late-night convos that refused to end at 11:30 when in full swing — even forgetting about the time. 🌙

Through misunderstandings we chose to talk through instead of run from. 💬

Through comfort. Through reassurance. Through choosing each other over and over. 💕

~ On Your Strength ~

You once said communication wasn't your strongest suit. But do you even realize how brave it is to open up the way you do? 💪

  • Those paras about your high school days
  • The vulnerable moments about your mental battles
  • The way you admit when you spiral
  • The way you try, even when you feel like you're failing
That's strength. That's someone worth celebrating.
You have no idea how much you matter. 💫

~ What You Mean To Me ~

  • You are the person I look forward to talking to
  • The one whose paragraphs I used to look forward to every morning no matter how chaotic my day was 📨
  • The notification that lightens my mood instantly ✨
  • The one whose silence I notice 🤍
  • The one whose well-being genuinely affects my day 💙
  • The one whose goodnight messages became part of my routine 🌙
  • The one whose selfies can send me into overkill mode like an idiot because I'm that head over heels for you 🥰

~ And Don't Get Me Started On You ~

👀 Your eyes? Dangerous levels of pretty.

😊 Your smile? Illegal — cuz it could fix an entire bad week.

💖 Your personality? Soft yet stubborn, realistic yet caring, independent yet worthy of being protected and spoiled.

The pictures you trusted me with, the parts of your past you shared, the struggles you admitted, the obsessions you "rambled" about — all of it feels like chapters of a story I'm honored to read. 📖

I meant it when I said you feel like a book series I'm hooked on. Every layer you unlock feels like a privilege and makes me keep waiting for more (even tho the hiatus has been bit long—) 😅💕

~ Your Journey This Year ~

I know this past year hasn't always been light for you. I know there were days you felt anti-social, silent days you felt disconnected. 🌧️

But you are still here.

  • Still trying
  • Still learning
  • Still evolving

And I'm so proud of you for that. 🌟

Remember that:

💙 You don't need to do a full 180 to deserve love

💙 You don't need to become easier to handle

💙 You don't need to lose yourself to keep people

Even during phases where time felt shorter, where routines shifted, where conversations felt chaotic — I never once doubted wanting you. If anything, those moments just showed me how much I value what we have — even when bits of realism and other overthinking thoughts tried to invade in the silence. 💭✨

~ Today, I Celebrate ~

So today, I don't just celebrate the cute girl I'm head over heels for as her biggest simp. 🙈💕

I celebrate:

  • The resilience you don't give yourself credit for 💪
  • The heart that loves intensely ❤️
  • The mind that overthinks but still chooses honesty 🧠
  • The girl who worries about being a burden while carrying so much herself 🌸

You are not hard to love. ❌

You are not a burden. ❌

You are not "too much". ❌

You are not lacking. ❌

BUT

💙 You are Leafa.

👑 You are my pride.

☀️ You are my sunshine.

🌟 You are the blessing I didn't know I needed.

~ Who You Are To Me ~

You are my companion. My best friend. My safe space. 🏠💕

The girl whose name lights up my notifications and somehow makes everything lighter. ✨

The one I miss in quiet moments to the point there's a huge hole of emptiness in my days. 💭

The one I want to be there for — not just on easy days, but on the heavy ones too. 🤍

~ My Wishes For You ~

On your birthday, I don't just wish you happiness. 🎂

  • I wish you peace in your own mind 🕊️
  • I wish you softness toward yourself 🌸
  • I wish you the confidence to know that being Leafa is already enough 💫
  • I wish this year brings you balance, growth, confidence, better health, and tons of moments that make you feel truly lucky to be alive 🌈

And selfishly? I wish to keep being part of your story. To see more chapters. To celebrate more birthdays. To eventually laugh about how our first ones were spent miles apart but still felt so full. 📖💙

~ If I Could Be There ~

Even if we can't spend today side by side, know this:

If I could, I would sit next to you, let you rest your head on my shoulder, and tell you in person how proud I am of you. 🤍

I'd remind you that the world is better because you exist. 🌍✨

That my world is better because you exist. 💙

~ Thank You ~

  • Thank you for trusting me 🤝
  • Thank you for choosing discussion over distance 💬
  • Thank you for letting me be someone you rely on 🏠
  • Thank you for being my companion, my safe space, my favorite notification 📱💕
  • Thank you for being the Leafa you are — without having to add filters or hide a part of yourself ✨

🎂 Happy Birthday to the most beautiful, cutest, sweetest, loveliest, complicated, strong, adorable, overthinking, soft-hearted princess I could've ever asked for. 👑💕

I love you. ❤️

Today, and on every day that follows till eternity. ♾️💙
